02 The Architecture

The 20n Trinity

Three pillars that fundamentally diverge from the Transformer paradigm.

01

Liquid Neural Networks

Instead of static layers, we use differential equations. The network is "liquid"—its parameters change during inference, not just training. It adapts without retraining.

02

Active Inference

We minimize Free Energy (Surprise) instead of maximizing reward. The agent has an internal world model and acts to make reality match predictions—like biological brains.
