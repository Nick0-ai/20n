Energy

The path

Four phases toward synthetic life. The first is underway. The last is speculative. Everything in between is engineering.

PHASE I — CURRENT

Silica Worm

A digital organism with fewer than 1,000 neurons. Learns through curiosity alone. No reward signal. Outperforming deep RL on navigation tasks.

PHASE II

Ghost in the Shell

Sim-to-real transfer. Physical robots that learn from single demonstrations. No fine-tuning. No cloud dependency.

PHASE III

Apex

Full anthropomorphic agents. Dexterous manipulation under 50 watts. Learning from fewer than three demonstrations.

PHASE IV — SPECULATIVE

Post-Human

Brain-computer interfaces. Synthetic biological tissues. The boundary between silicon and carbon becomes irrelevant.
