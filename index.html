The wall

The AI industry is racing toward four terminal asymptotes. Everyone sees them coming. Few are building alternatives.

01

Energy

Scaling transformers requires exponentially more power. By 2030, AI could consume more electricity than small countries.

02

Data

The internet is finite. Quality text is exhausted. Synthetic data leads to model collapse. The well is running dry.

03

Latency

Cloud inference takes 200-500ms. A robot cannot wait. Real-time autonomy requires edge intelligence.

04

Reality

Language models have no causal understanding. They predict text, not physics. They hallucinate the world.

The trinity

ARCHITECTURE

Liquid Networks

Networks where time constants adapt during inference. Not frozen weights—flowing dynamics that keep learning after deployment.

dx/dt = f(x, τ(input))
OBJECTIVE

Active Inference

Agents that minimize surprise rather than maximize reward. They build world models and act to reduce uncertainty. Curiosity is built in.

argmin F(q, y)
SUBSTRATE

Sparse Signaling

Information transmitted only when needed—through events, not continuous streams. Orders of magnitude more efficient than dense computation.

~0.5W / chip

The perception-action loop

Traditional AI is a one-way street: input → process → output. Living systems work differently. They continuously predict, act, and update.

  • World model predicts sensory input
  • Prediction errors drive learning
  • Actions are chosen to minimize surprise
  • The loop never stops—even after deployment
03

The path

Four phases. Subject to revision. The first is already underway.

PHASE I — CURRENT

Silica Worm

Digital organism with <1,000 parameters. Learns to navigate through curiosity alone. No reward signal. Outperforming deep RL baselines.

PHASE II — NEXT

Embodiment

Sim-to-real transfer. Physical robots that learn new tasks from single demonstrations. No fine-tuning. No cloud.

PHASE III — HORIZON

Apex

Full anthropomorphic agents. Dexterous manipulation. <50W continuous. Learning from <3 demonstrations.

PHASE IV — SPECULATIVE

Post-Human

Brain-computer interfaces. Synthetic biological tissues. Consciousness extension. This phase is speculative.
